FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before registering.

Who is eligible to register?
Any child aged 5 to 17 residing in Sri Lanka is eligible. Select the age group that matches your child's current age at the time of registration. One registration per NIC number is allowed.
What is the aptitude exam?
It is a 30-minute online multiple-choice exam taken immediately after OTP-verified registration. Questions are age-appropriate (15–40 MCQs depending on group) and available in Sinhala, Tamil, and English. Your score determines your scholarship tier — no preparation is required.
How is the scholarship applied?
Immediately after your exam, your results page will show your score, tier (Gold / Silver / Bronze), and the exact discounted fee. A printable payment slip is generated automatically. Bring this to your nearest AIC centre to complete enrolment.
What if my child scores below 60%?
A score below 60% does not qualify for a scholarship discount, but your child is still very welcome to enrol at the full course fee. The programme is designed to nurture all learners regardless of their initial aptitude level.
Are monthly instalments available?
Yes. Monthly instalment plans are available for all age groups. Please inquire at your nearest AIC centre for the specific instalment schedule after your scholarship is confirmed.
What certificate will my child receive?
Upon successfully completing the full 40-week programme, students receive an internationally recognised Certificate of Completion issued by TWL (The Worldwide Learning), United Kingdom. This certificate is recognised globally.
Can the exam be taken in Sinhala or Tamil?
Yes. The aptitude exam portal fully supports Sinhala (සිංහල), Tamil (தமிழ்), and English. You can switch the language at any time using the language switcher at the top of the exam page.
